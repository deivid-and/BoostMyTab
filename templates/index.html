Getting Started with BoostMyTab

Follow these steps to connect your tablet and use BoostMyTab effectively:

Step 1: Enable Developer Options

Navigate to Settings > About Tablet > Software Information. Tap on Build Number seven times to unlock Developer Options.

Step 2: Enable USB Debugging

In Settings, go to Developer Options and turn on USB Debugging.

Step 3: Connect Your Tablet to the PC

Connect your tablet to your PC using a USB cable. When prompted on your tablet, authorize USB debugging by tapping Authorize.

Step 4: Launch BoostMyTab

Once connected, start running optimization scripts using BoostMyTab. If you want to mirror and control your tablet from your PC, click on the Connect Tab to PC button.

Step 5: Troubleshooting Tips

If you encounter any issues, ensure that:

  • Your tablet is properly connected to the PC.
  • Developer Options are enabled.
  • USB Debugging is turned on and has been authorized.
  • All unnecessary background applications are closed for optimal performance.

After verifying these settings, you should be ready to use BoostMyTab without issues.
